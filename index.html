Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Best Trunk contest work?

Voting for the best trunk will take place during the event. Just follow the instructions onsite to cast your vote for your favorite! Winners will be announced at 5:30 PM.

What time should trunk participants arrive to set up?

If you have arranged to decorate a trunk, please arrive between 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM to get parked and set up. All vehicles must be in place by 3:15 PM for safety.
