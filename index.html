Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter the Best Trunk contest?

Entry is automatic for all registered trunks! Our secret judges will be roaming the event. Winners will be announced at 5:30 PM on the main stage.

What time should trunk participants arrive?

Please arrive between 2:30 PM and 3:15 PM to get parked and set up. All vehicles must be in place by 3:15 PM for safety.
