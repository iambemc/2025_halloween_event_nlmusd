About The Event

Our annual Trunk-or-Treat provides a safe and exciting environment for children and families to enjoy Halloween. Lampton families and staff decorate their vehicle trunks and hand out candy as kids "trick-or-treat" from car to car. It's a fantastic evening of creative decorations, costumes, and school spirit. Come see the amazing displays and collect some sweet treats!

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if it rains?

In case of bad weather, our rain date is scheduled for the next day, Sunday, October 26th, at the same time and location. We will notify all registered participants via email.

How do I enter the costume contest?

Entry is automatic! Just be near the main stage at 6:00 PM when our judges are looking for the best costumes. Good luck!

What time should trunk participants arrive to set up?

If you are decorating a trunk, please plan to arrive between 2:30 PM and 3:30 PM to get parked and set up. All vehicles must be in place by 3:45 PM for safety.
